About Trafford Plumbing & DIY

We're Trafford Plumbing & DIY, proudly serving Old Trafford and the wider Manchester area from our store on Ayres Road. Whether you're a seasoned tradesperson stocking up on plumbing supplies or a homeowner tackling a weekend project, we've got the products and the know-how to help you get the job done right.

Pop in and see us — we're always happy to offer advice and find the right solution for your needs. As Old Trafford's go-to destination for plumbing supplies and DIY essentials, we offer expert guidance and quality products for both trade professionals and home improvers.
